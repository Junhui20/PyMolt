# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
PyMolt community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
publishing others' private information, and other conduct that could reasonably
be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project
maintainers through GitHub. Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards
and will take appropriate, fair corrective action in response to any behavior
they deem inappropriate.
